Our Story

Brewing passion since 2010

SteamyCup began as a small dream in a cozy corner of the city. What started as a humble coffee cart has grown into a community hub where coffee lovers gather to share stories, ideas, and perfect brews.

Our founder, Maria, spent years traveling through coffee regions around the world, learning from farmers and roasters. She brought back not just beans, but a philosophy: every cup should tell a story and create connection.

Today, we continue that tradition by sourcing ethical beans, roasting in small batches, and training our baristas in the art of perfect preparation.
